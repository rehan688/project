Diner Support

Having trouble with an order? We're here to help you get it resolved quickly.

Order Issues? Contact the Restaurant First

For the fastest resolution, please contact the restaurant directly. They have the ability to issue refunds, correct orders, and resolve most issues immediately.

I was charged but didn't receive my order

Please contact the restaurant directly. If they're unable to resolve it, reach out to us with your order confirmation number and we'll investigate.

My order was incorrect or missing items

Contact the restaurant right away. They can often send a driver back or issue a credit for your next order.

My order is taking too long

Delivery times can vary based on demand, weather, and distance. Check your order status for real-time updates. If it's significantly delayed, call the restaurant.

I want to cancel my order

Contact the restaurant immediately. If the order hasn't been prepared yet, they can cancel and issue a full refund.

I'm having trouble placing an order

Try clearing your browser cache, using a different browser, or downloading the restaurant's app. If issues persist, email us at support@chownow.com.

Still Need Help?

Email our diner support team and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
